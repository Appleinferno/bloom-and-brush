Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book face painting for a birthday party?

We recommend booking at least three to four weeks ahead, especially for weekend dates during spring and fall. Those tend to fill up first.

How many kids can you paint per hour?

For speed designs like cheek art, we can do around 20 to 25 faces per hour. Full-face designs take a bit longer, usually around 5 to 7 minutes each.

Is the face paint safe for sensitive skin?

Yes. We only use water-based, hypoallergenic, FDA-compliant face paints made specifically for use on skin. They wash off easily with soap and water.

Do you travel outside of North Georgia?

We regularly serve Western North Carolina and Southeast Tennessee as well. If you're within about an hour of Blairsville, we can likely make it work.

What do I need to provide at the party?

Just a shaded spot if we're outdoors and a table if we're inside. We bring everything else, including our own chair, supplies, and cleanup materials.

Can adults get their faces painted too?

Absolutely. We paint all ages. Adults usually go for cheek designs or small arm art, but full-face looks are always welcome.
